# Saqqara (The Step Pyramid)

**Built:** ~2700 BC

The world's oldest monumental masonry structure — the oldest stone construction ever built. Saqqara served as the necropolis of the ancient Egyptian capital Memphis. The famous Step Pyramid dates to King Djoser of the 3rd dynasty and was designed by the legendary architect Imhotep.

**Location:** 30 miles (19 km) south of Cairo.
**Hours:** Daily 9 a.m. – 5 p.m.
**Getting there:** About an hour from Cairo city center by car.
