# The Unfinished Obelisk

The largest obelisk ever attempted in ancient Egypt — left unfinished in the granite quarry due to natural cracks that appeared in the stone. Had the ancient Egyptians managed to extract and erect it, it would have been the single heaviest piece of stone in history, weighing approximately 1,167 tonnes.

Visitors to the quarry can see the marks of other obelisks and artifacts that were successfully extracted and transported to temples across Egypt — a rare look at the construction methods behind the monuments you see everywhere else.

**Location:** Aswan, about 1.5 km from the town center, across from an old Fatimid Cemetery.
**Hours:** Daily 7 a.m. – 4 p.m.
**Getting there:** About 10–15 minutes by car from Aswan.
