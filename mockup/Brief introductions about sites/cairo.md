# Cairo

The capital of Egypt and the largest city in Africa, the Arab World and the Middle East. With a population that exceeds 20 million, it is one of the most densely populated cities in the world. Its history goes back to roughly 2000 BC when Memphis (15 miles southeast of modern Cairo) was founded as a capital of Egypt and housed the famous Giza Pyramids.

Located by the Delta of the River Nile, modern Cairo was founded by the Fatimid dynasty in 969 AD. A city that never sleeps — you can enjoy sightseeing across different historical eras (Ancient Egypt, Coptic, and Islamic history), plus everything a great modern city has to offer.

**Main attractions:** The Giza Pyramids & The Sphinx, Saqqara, Memphis, The Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM), The Citadel, and Coptic Cairo.
