**DAY-BY-DAY ITINERARY**

**Day 1**

**Cairo**

**Arrival & Welcome**

Upon your arrival at Cairo International Airport, a dedicated representative will be waiting to greet you and facilitate a seamless, private transfer to your five-star hotel. Following check-in, you will meet your private Egyptologist for a personalized orientation. This session provides an overview of your upcoming itinerary, helpful local insights, and an opportunity to address any questions. The remainder of the day is yours to relax and enjoy dinner at your leisure within the hotel.

**Day 2**

**Cairo --- Pharaonic**

**Giza Pyramids, Sphinx & Grand Egyptian Museum**

We begin the day with an early departure to the Giza Plateau, allowing you to experience these iconic landmarks before the peak crowds arrive. Your expert Egyptologist will provide deep historical context, moving beyond the surface to explore the purpose and cultural significance of these architectural marvels through stories rarely captured by standard audio guides. Following a curated lunch, we will proceed to the newly opened Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM) for an immersive tour of the complete Tutankhamun collection.

**Day 3 Cairo --- Islamic & Coptic**

**Citadel, Mohammed Ali Mosque, Coptic Cairo, NMEC museum -- Fly to Luxor.**

Your day begins with a visit to the Saladin Citadel and the exquisite Mosque of Muhammad Ali, where you will enjoy the most panoramic view of Cairo available within the city. We then proceed to explore the ancient, storied streets of Coptic Cairo, home to one of the oldest Christian communities in the world. Following a curated lunch, we will visit the National Museum of Egyptian Civilization (NMEC); final home of the most famous royal mummies. In the afternoon, you will be transferred to the airport for your flight to Luxor, where you will check into your hotel to rest and prepare for the adventures awaiting you the following day.

**Day 4**

**Luxor (East Bank Tour)**

Your journey continues with your private guide who will escort you to a highly acclaimed local restaurant for an authentic lunch. The afternoon features a tour of the monumental Karnak Temple Complex, followed by an evening visit to the Luxor Temple. Experiencing the temple illuminated at night offers a breathtaking and distinct perspective, providing a completely different atmosphere compared to a daytime visit.

**Day 5\
Luxor (West Bank Tour) -- Edfu Temple -- Arrive Aswan**

**Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple, The Artisans' Village & Edfu Temple.**

Enjoy a comprehensive exploration of the West Bank, beginning with the Valley of the Kings, where you will visit three significant tombs, plus the extraordinary final resting place of Ramesses V and VI. Your tour continues with visits to the Temple of Hatshepsut, the remarkable Artisans\' Village (Deir el-Medina), and the towering Colossi of Memnon. A curated lunch is included during your day of discovery. Then your private driver will drive you to Edfu where you will visit Edfu Temple; one of Egypt's most spectacularly preserved monuments with its massive breathtaking pylon and soaring columns. Continue driving to Aswan and check in at your hotel.

**Day 6**

**Aswan Tour & The Nubian Village, fly to Cairo.**

Today, you will embark on a memorable excursion with your tour guide to the breathtaking Philae Temple, a stunning island sanctuary dedicated to the goddess Isis that you will reach via a scenic private boat ride. After immersing yourself in the magic of this ancient wonder, you will visit the impressive Aswan High Dam and the fascinating Unfinished Obelisk. Next, enjoy a delicious lunch at a restaurant right along the banks of the Nile before exploring the vibrant and colorful Nubian Village. Finally, you will be comfortably transferred to Aswan Airport to catch your flight back to Cairo where our representatives will pick you up and transfer you to your airport hotel; check in and enjoy peaceful night sleep.

**Day 7**

**Final Departure**

Our company representative will meet you at your hotel to transfer you to the departure terminal, providing full luggage assistance until you are safely checked in at the airline desk.
