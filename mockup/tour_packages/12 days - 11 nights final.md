**Day-by-Day Itinerary**

**Day 1**

**Cairo**

**Arrival & Welcome**

**Your dedicated representative will meet you at Cairo International Airport with a name sign. Private transfer to your 5-star hotel. Check-in and meet your Egyptologist guide for a brief orientation --- what to expect, tips for the days ahead, and any questions answered. Rest of the day free. Dinner on your own at the hotel.  Overnight in Cairo. **

**Day 2**

**Cairo --- Pharaonic**

**Giza Pyramids, the Sphinx & The Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM).**

**We begin the day with an early departure to the Giza Plateau, allowing you to experience these iconic landmarks before the peak crowds arrive. Your expert Egyptologist will provide deep historical context, moving beyond the surface to explore the purpose and cultural significance of these architectural marvels through stories rarely captured by standard audio guides. Following a curated lunch, we will proceed to the newly opened Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM) for an immersive tour of the complete Tutankhamun collection. Overnight in Cairo.**

**Day 3**

**Cairo --- Islamic & Coptic history - Museum -- Fly to Luxor**

**Citadel, Mohammed Ali Mosque & Coptic Cairo, NMEC**

**Your day begins with a visit to the Saladin Citadel and the exquisite Mosque of Muhammad Ali, where you will enjoy the most panoramic view of Cairo available within the city. We then proceed to explore the ancient, storied streets of Coptic Cairo, home to one of the oldest Christian communities in the world. Following a curated lunch, we will visit the National Museum of Egyptian Civilization (NMEC); final home of the most famous royal mummies. In the afternoon, you will be transferred to Cairo airport for your flight to Luxor (an hour flight) where you will be assisted by our representative until you check into your hotel to rest and prepare for the adventures awaiting you the following day. Overnight in Luxor.**

**Day 4**

**Luxor - East Bank Tour -- Nile Cruise Boarding**

**Karnak Temples, Luxor Temple & The Sphinx Avenue.**

**Have breakfast then check out of your hotel and join your tour guide to visit Karnak Temples; The largest monumental complex in the world which is known as the history book of Ancient Egypt then head to your cruise ship for check in and lunch. Bypassing Luxor Temple daytime deliberately because it won't be just the history but we invite you to experience Luxor Temple and The Sphinx Avenue bathed in the golden glow of twilight; a totally extraordinary experience. Dinner on the ship. Overnight in Luxor.**

**Day 5**

**Luxor --- West Bank Tour**

**The Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple, The Village of the artisans**

**Early breakfast on the ship then join your tour guide to the heart of the mountains of western Thebes where Egypt's best secret depicting the afterlife of the Pharaohs with dazzling, untouched colors and myths that refuse to fade. You will visit 3 royal tombs plus the extraordinary tomb of Ramesses V & VI. Followed by Hatshepsut's Temple rising majestically out of the desert cliffs; a breathtaking triumph of ancient architecture where the most powerful lady of Ancient Egypt's history built her mortuary temple. Our third site takes you off the Pharaohs' track into the ancient world's ultimate artisan village; this is ancient Egypt at its most human where you can see the ruins of their village and intact tombs. Finally, we will stop at the colossi of Memnon on our way back to the cruise ship. Back on board, lunch, the cruise sails smoothly down the Nile, offering front-row seats to a timeless landscape of riverside villages, roaming farm animals, and swaying palms. The journey pauses for a fascinating crossing through the Esna Lock until the ship docks at Edfu. Overnight in Edfu.**

**Day 6: Edfu and Com Ombo**

**Edfu - Com Ombo Temples**

**Breakfast on board, then join your tour guide to visit Egypt's most preserved ancient sanctuary where time has stood completely still for over two millennia; walk through the massive gateways, towering roofs of the falcon headed deity Horus of Edfu. Back to the boat, lunch while sailing with the marvelous Nile scenery around. Docking at Com Ombo in the afternoon, then your tour guide will accompany you to visit Egypt's only twin temple shadowed by the sunlight that was dedicated to two deities. Continue sailing until you dock in Aswan. Dinner on board. Overnight in Aswan.**

**Day 7:   Aswan Tour**

**Aswan -- Philae Temple -- The Unfinished Obelisk -- The High Dam**

**Breakfast on board, then join your tour guide for a visit to the pearl of Egypt where you get on a boat and cross over to the floating sanctuary of Isis, surrounded by water on all sides feels like stepping into a living postcard. Followed by The High Dam; a rock-filled dam overlooking Lake Nasser; the largest manmade lake in the world. Finally, we will visit the ancient quarry of granite where the Unfinished Obelisk is still laying; the largest and heaviest in the world. Overnight in Aswan.**

**Day 8**

**Free relaxing day with all meals on the cruise ship.** *Optional excursion to visit Abu Simble Temples;* *the colossal temples of Ramesses II carved into a cliff face --- one of Egypt\'s most extraordinary sights. ***Overnight in Aswan.**

**Day 9**

**The Nubian Village - Sailing Day**

**After breakfast on board, embark on a scenic motorboat ride to the vibrant Nubian Village. A photographer's paradise, this village is a visual feast of narrow streets, welcoming locals, and homes painted in brilliant rainbow colors and geometric patterns. Return to the ship for lunch as we set sail from Aswan. Spend the afternoon taking in the timeless riverside scenery: local farmers tending to their livestock against a striking backdrop where golden desert sands meet lush green fields and the deep blue Nile. Dinner on board. Overnight in Edfu.**

**Day 10**

**Sailing The Nile - Esna Lock- Luxor City Tour.**

**Wake up to breakfast on board as we set sail from Edfu, taking in the spectacular scenery along the Nile. Experience a fascinating engineering marvel as our ship passes through the Esna Lock, safely lowering our water level to continue the journey north. After lunch on deck, we arrive in historic Luxor. Step ashore for an enchanting city tour by horse-drawn carriage---winding through bustling local markets, passing the legendary Avenue of Sphinxes, and soaking up the vibrant, timeless energy of the city streets as locals go about their day. Dinner on board, overnight in Luxor.**

**Day 11: West Bank Tour (2) -- Fly to Cairo**

**The Valley of The Queens -- Madinet Habu Temple -- The Valley of the Nobles.**

**Begin the day with breakfast on board before joining your private guide to explore Luxor's best-kept secrets. Step off the beaten path into extraordinary, lesser-visited historical sites where ancient pharaonic secrets come to life through remarkably vivid, untouched colors and astonishing relief walls, we will visit 3 hidden gems as part of the ancient royal cemetery of Luxor. After a curated lunch at a highly rated local restaurant, our representative will transfer you to Luxor Airport and assist with your luggage for your flight back to Cairo. Upon arrival, we will greet you at the terminal and handle your private transfer to your airport hotel for a peaceful night\'s rest. Overnight in Cairo.**

**Day 12: Final Departure**

**Enjoy breakfast at the hotel followed by checkout. Our representative will meet you to provide a private transfer to your departure terminal, remaining with you to assist with your luggage until you are safely checked in at the airline's desk.**
