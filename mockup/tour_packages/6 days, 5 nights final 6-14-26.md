Day-by-Day Itinerary
Day 1
Cairo
Arrival & Welcome
Upon your arrival at Cairo International Airport, a dedicated representative will be waiting to greet you and facilitate a seamless, private transfer to your five-star hotel. Following check-in, you will meet your private Egyptologist for a personalized orientation. This session provides an overview of your upcoming itinerary, helpful local insights, and an opportunity to address any questions. The remainder of the day is yours to relax and enjoy dinner at your leisure within the hotel.
Day 2
Cairo — Pharaonic
Giza Pyramids, Sphinx & Grand Egyptian Museum
We begin the day with an early departure to the Giza Plateau, allowing you to experience these iconic landmarks before the peak crowds arrive. Your expert Egyptologist will provide deep historical context, moving beyond the surface to explore the purpose and cultural significance of these architectural marvels through stories rarely captured by standard audio guides. Following a curated lunch, we will proceed to the newly opened Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM) for an immersive tour of the complete Tutankhamun collection.




Day 3 Cairo — Islamic & Coptic
Citadel, Mohammed Ali Mosque, Coptic Cairo, NMEC museum-fly to Luxor
Your day begins with a visit to the Saladin Citadel and the exquisite Mosque of Muhammad Ali, where you will enjoy the most panoramic view of Cairo available within the city. We then proceed to explore the ancient, storied streets of Coptic Cairo, home to one of the oldest Christian communities in the world. Following a curated lunch, we will visit the National Museum of Egyptian Civilization (NMEC). In the afternoon, you will be transferred to the airport for your flight to Luxor, where you will check into your hotel to rest and prepare for the adventures awaiting you the following day.

Day 4 Cairo → Luxor
East Bank Tour
Your journey continues with your private guide who will escort you to a highly acclaimed local restaurant for an authentic lunch. The afternoon features a tour of the monumental Karnak Temple Complex, followed by an evening visit to the Luxor Temple. Experiencing the temple illuminated at night offers a breathtaking and distinct perspective, providing a completely different atmosphere compared to a daytime visit.
Day 5
Luxor — West Bank Tour
Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple, The Artisans’ Village & Nile Felucca
Enjoy a comprehensive exploration of the West Bank, beginning with the Valley of the Kings, where you will visit three significant tombs, plus the extraordinary final resting place of Ramesses V and VI. Your tour continues with visits to the Temple of Hatshepsut, the remarkable Artisans' Village (Deir el-Medina), and the towering Colossi of Memnon. A curated lunch is included during your day of discovery. As the afternoon concludes, experience a relaxing sunset felucca ride along the Nile. Afterward, you will be transferred to Luxor Airport with the assistance of our representative to board your flight to Cairo. Upon arrival in Cairo, our representative will meet you at the airport to facilitate a seamless transition and assist with your check-in at your airport hotel for an overnight stay.
Day 6
Checkout & Final Departure.
Our representative will pick you up from the hotel, transfer you to your departure terminal and assist you with the luggage until you check in at the airline desk.
Day 6:
Checkout and Final Departure
Following your checkout, our representative will collect you from your hotel and provide a private transfer to your departure terminal. To ensure a stress-free conclusion to your journey, our team will remain with you to provide full assistance with your luggage until you have successfully checked in at your airline’s departure desk.
