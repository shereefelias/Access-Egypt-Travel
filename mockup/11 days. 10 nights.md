Day-by-Day Itinerary
Day 1
Cairo
Arrival & Welcome
Your dedicated representative will meet you at Cairo International Airport with a name sign. Private transfer to your 5-star hotel. Check-in and meet your Egyptologist guide for a brief orientation — what to expect, tips for the days ahead, and any questions answered. Rest of the day free. Dinner on your own at the hotel.  Overnight in Cairo.
Day 2
Cairo —
Giza Pyramids, the Sphinx & The Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM).
An early start to beat the crowds, you will join your tour guide to visit the Giza Plateau. You will stand in front of the astonishing Pyramids and the legendary Sphinx  . Lunch included. Afternoon: The Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM), newly fully open — home to the complete Tutankhamun collection. Overnight in Cairo.
Day 3
Cairo — Islamic & Coptic history – Fly to Luxor
Citadel, Mohammed Ali Mosque & Coptic Cairo
Saladin Citadel and the beautiful Mohammed Ali alabaster Mosque — the best view of Cairo from any point in the city. Then the ancient churches and streets of Coptic Cairo, one of the oldest Christian communities in the world where The Holly Family stayed. National Museum of Egyptian Civilization (NMEC) ;home to the most famous royal mummies. Lunch included. Transfer to Cairo airport, then fly to Luxor (around an hour flight) .  Transfer by our representative to your hotel in Luxor. Overnight in Luxor.

Day 4
East Bank Tour
Have breakfast then check out of your hotel and join your tour guide to visit Karnak Temples; The largest monumental complex in the world which is known as the history book of Ancient Egypt then head to your cruise ship for check in and lunch. Bypassing Luxor Temple daytime deliberately because it won’t be just the history but we invite you to experience Luxor Temple bathed in the golden glow of twilight."; a totally extraordinary experience. Dinner on the ship. Overnight in Luxor.

Day 5
Luxor — West Bank (We need it in blue so we can add a brief).
Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple, The Village of the artisans
Early breakfast on the ship then join your tour guide to the heart of the mountains of western Thebes where Egypt’s best secret depicting the afterlife of the Pharaohs with dazzling, untouched colors and myths that refuse to fade. You will visit 3 royal tombs plus the extraordinary tomb of Ramesses 5th & 6th. Followed by Hatshepsut’s Temple rising majestically out of the desert cliffs; a breathtaking triumph of ancient architecture where the most powerful lady of Ancient Egypt’s history built her mortuary temple. Our third site takes you off the Pharaohs’ track into  the ancient world’s ultimate artisan village; this is ancient Egypt at its most human where you can see the ruins of their village and intact tombs. Finally we will stop at the colossi of Memnon on our way back to the cruise ship. Lunch on board then enjoy the rest of your day sailing down The Nile, passing through the Esna Lock until you dock at Edfu at night. Dinner on board. Overnight in Edfu.

Day 6
Edfu & Com Ombo Temples
Breakfast on board, then join your tour guide to visit Egypt’s most preserved ancient sanctuary where time has stood completely still for over two millennia; walk through the massive gateways, towering roofs of the falcon headed deity Horus of Edfu. Back to the boat, set sail with the marvelous Nile scenery around. Docking at Com Ombo in the afternoon, then your tour guide will accompany you to visit Egypt’s only twin temple shadowed by the sunlight that was dedicated to two deities. Continue sailing until you dock in Aswan. Dinner on board. Overnight in Aswan.

Day 7
Aswan – Philae Temple – The Unfinished Obelisk – The High Dam – The Nubian Village
Breakfast on board, then join your tour guide for a visit to the pearl of Egypt where you get on a boat and cross over to the floating sanctuary of Isis, surrounded by water on all sides feels like stepping into a living postcard. Followed by The High Dam; a rock-filled dam overlooking Lake Nasser; the largest manmade lake in the world. Finally we will visit the ancient quarry of granite where the Unfinished Obelisk is still laying; the largest and heaviest in the world.
Lunch on board, then a special visit by motor boat to the Nubian Village; a photographer’s dream, with its tiny streets, Nubian people and rainbow-painted house and geometrical designs; it’s a visual feast for the eyes. Dinner on board. Overnight in Aswan.

Day 8
Free day with all meals on the cruise ship.  Optional excursion to visit Abu Simble Temples. Overnight in Aswan.
Day 9
Sailing day northward to Luxor. Overnight in

Today we check out of the cruise ship; our representative will assist you with the luggage and transfer you to Aswan airport for your flight to Cairo. Lunch is included either in Aswan or Cairo depending on the time your flight departs. Arrive Cairo, our representative will be waiting for you and transfer you to your airport hotel.
Day 9
Breakfast at the hotel, our representative will pick you up and assist you with the luggage until your final departure.
