Day-by-Day Itinerary
Day 1
Cairo Arrival
Your dedicated representative will meet you at Cairo International Airport with a name sign. Private transfer to your 5-star hotel. Check-in and meet your Egyptologist guide for a brief orientation — what to expect, tips for the days ahead, and any questions answered. Rest of the day free. Dinner on your own maybe we should add (at the hotel) just to let clients feel at ease.  Overnight in Cairo.
Day 2
Cairo —
Giza Pyramids, the Sphinx & Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM).
An early start to beat the crowds, you will join your tour guide to visit the Giza Plateau. You will stand in front of the astonishing Pyramids and the legendary Sphinx  . Lunch included. Afternoon: the Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM), newly fully open — home to the complete Tutankhamun collection. Overnight in Cairo.
Day 3
Cairo — Islamic & Coptic history – Fly to Aswan
Citadel, Mohammed Ali Mosque & Coptic Cairo
Saladin Citadel and the beautiful Mohammed Ali alabaster Mosque — the best view of Cairo from any point in the city. Then the ancient churches and streets of Coptic Cairo, one of the oldest Christian communities in the world where The Holly Family stayed. National Museum of Egyptian Civilization (NMEC) ;home to the most famous royal mummies. Lunch included. Transfer to Cairo airport, then fly to Aswan (around an hour and half flight) .  Transfer by our representative to your hotel in Aswan. Overnight in Aswan

Day 4
Aswan Tour
Visit Philae Temple by a motor-boat that takes you to the island where Isis was deified, followed by The Unfinished Obelisk which is the largest in the world, and The High Dam then Check in on your cruise ship, have lunch . Overnight in Aswan.

Day 5
Felucca and Com Ombo Temple.
Join your tour guide for a felucca ride around the ancient island of Elephantine . Then back to the cruise ship for lunch and set sail enjoying the scenery of the majestic Nile until the ship docks at Com Ombo, then you will join your tour guide to visit Com Ombo Temple which is a short walk from the cruise ship. Continue sailing and dock at Edfu. Overnight in Edfu.

Day 6
Edfu Temple – East Bank Tour

Early morning to visit Edfu Temple; the most intact temple of ancient Egypt, then back to the boat and set sail northward towards Luxor passing through the Esna Lock where the boat has to be lowered for about 30 feet. Docking in Luxor in the afternoon then go visit Karnak Temples; the largest monumental complex in the world, followed by Luxor Temple(lit up in the evening , a totally different experience). Overnight in Luxor.
Day 6
Luxor — West Bank & Fly to Cairo.

Check out of your cruise ship, then visit The Valley of the Kings with its vivid colors ( 3 tombs plus the extraordinary tomb of Ramesses 5th&6th), Temple of Hatshepsut, the artisans' village at Deir el-Medina (exclusively for our clients) , and the Colossi of Memnon. Followed by lunch then our representative will pick you up for a private transfer to Luxor Airport for your onward flight to Cairo. Upon landing in Cairo our representative will meet you and assist with your luggage and transfer to your airport hotel. Overnight in Cairo.

Day 7
Final Departure
Breakfast at the hotel, then our representative will pick you up , assist with your luggage and transfer you to your departure terminal at Cairo airport.
